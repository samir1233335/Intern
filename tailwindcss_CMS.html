Who We Are

Committed to Exceptional Patient Care

Founded in 1998, MedCore Hospital has grown into a leading multi-specialty healthcare institution. We combine clinical expertise with a patient-centred philosophy to deliver care that truly makes a difference.

Our Mission

To provide safe, effective, and compassionate healthcare to every patient, regardless of background or condition.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted healthcare provider, setting benchmarks in clinical excellence and patient satisfaction.

Our Values

Integrity, empathy, innovation, and teamwork guide everything we do at MedCore.

What We Offer

Our Medical Services

From routine check-ups to complex surgeries, our team delivers comprehensive care across every discipline.

Cardiology

Advanced diagnostics and treatments for all heart conditions with state-of-the-art cath labs.

Neurology

Comprehensive brain, spine, and nerve disorder care with MRI-guided precision therapies.

Orthopedics

Joint replacement, sports medicine, and spinal surgery from expert orthopedic surgeons.

Pediatrics

Dedicated child health services from newborns to adolescents in a welcoming environment.

Oncology

Multidisciplinary cancer care including chemotherapy, radiation, and immunotherapy programs.

Ophthalmology

LASIK, cataract surgery, and retinal treatments using the latest laser technologies.

Pulmonology

Expert management of asthma, COPD, sleep disorders, and respiratory infections.

Endocrinology

Diabetes care, thyroid management, and hormonal disorder treatments tailored to your needs.
